We will repeat this conversation twice. Checkup is probably a new word.

  • A: Hello. I'm Dr. Medina. Why are you here today?
  • B: I'm here for a checkup.
  • A: Ok. Sit on the table. Look straight ahead.

Listen and read

We will repeat this conversation twice, then copy this conversation into the chat.

Isabel gets a checkup every year. She goes to the health clinic. Her appointment is on Monday. Her doctor says, "You are healthy."

True or false

Put the students in breakout rooms. Ask them whether the statement is true or false.

  • Isabel gets a checkup every year.
  • Isabel has an appointment on Tuesday.
  • Her appointment is at the school.
